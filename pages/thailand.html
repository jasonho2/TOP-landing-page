Thailand
Activities

    Fly into Bangkok Intl Airport

  1. Central/Bangkok - city and history vibes:
    1. Siam Square for most lively and shopping
    2. Sukhumvit for bars, restaurants, shopping centers, and more
    3. Khao San for night life
    4. Old Town for historical sights and temples
    5. Silom for rooftop bars, street vendors, sidewalk stores
    6. Hike and Swim - Erawan Waterfalls, Khao Yai National Park
    7. Temples and Shrines
      1. Wat Phra Kaew (The Grand Palace)
      2. Wat Pho Phra
      3. Phraya Nakhon Cave and Temple

    3. Fly out of Chiang Mai Intl Airport to Phuket

  3. Southern/Phuket - beach, nature, and island vibes:
      West - Phuket, Phi Phi Islands, Krabi
    1. Phang Nga Bay / James Bond Island
    2. Khao Sok National Park, Cheow Lan Lake, and Elephant Hill
    3. Big Buddha @ Chalong, and a great view of Phuket
    4. Bang La Road in Pa Tong - nightlife, bars, shisha, ping pong show lol and more
    5. Old Town to eat, shop, and explore
    6. Rassada Port/Pier can shuttle us to the Ko Phi Phi Islands. Visit Maya Bay via long tail boat tour. Sample activities include beach, caves, and snorkeling. Sunset at "Viewpoint 3" and watch a fire show on the beach

    1. Ang Thong Marine National Park

Lodging
  1. Carlton Hotel in Sukhumvit for one room is ~$136-$151 per night. Two (or more...?) people sharing a room would be ideal and save on cost. Most mid-luxury hotels seems to be in the range of ~$130-$300 per night if booked early enough.
  2. Luxury villas in Phuket, Krabi, Koh Pha-Ngan, and Ko Samui offer spaces for upwards of 18 people for $700-$2500/night (total cost), many of which offer an included private chef.
