    5. Silom for rooftop bars, street vendors, sidewalk stores
    6. Hike and Swim - Erawan Waterfalls, Khao Yai National Park
    7. Temples and Shrines
      1. Wat Phra Kaew (The Grand Palace)
      2. Wat Pho Phra

Lodging (examples)
  1. Carlton Hotel in Sukhumvit for one room is ~$136-$151 per night. Two (or more...?) people sharing a room would be ideal and save on cost. Most mid-luxury hotels seems to be in the range of ~$130-$300 per night if booked early enough.
  2. AirBnB villa in Chiang Mai for 10 guests and 3 nights (Dec 2 - Dec 5, 2025) is $2441, roughly $81/person. This is one of the more expensive options, too...
  3. AirBnB home and bugalows in Chiang Rai for 2 nights (Dec 5 - Dec 7) comes out to $768, or $38/person assuming 10 people. Other hotels in the area are in the $60-$230 range for 2 guests and 2 nights (or $15-$58 per person).
  4. Luxury villas in Phuket, Krabi, Koh Pha-Ngan, and Ko Samui offer spaces for upwards of 18 people for $700-$2500/night, many of which offer an included private chef.
  5. Rooms in Patong at Lub d Hotel for 2-4 people as much as $225 per night, or at most ~$50-$60 per person. Hotel is one block walking distance to Bang La Road.
Transit
  1. River taxi (Bangkok): fares range from $0.45-$2 for single trips, $3.30-$10 per day, or $6.60-$13.30 for a 3-day pass. Some local ferries even run at $0.15 per trip. A handy handy map and blog summarizing how to get around Bangkok via river taxi.
  2. BTS Skytrain: maximum single fare is $1.75. One day ticket costs $4.44.
  3. Bus: also cheap
  4. Tuk tuk: can be a few USD but like taxis, they could try to scam you by taking advantage of tourists by taking longer routes or asking for higher prices.
  5. Taxi
  6. Train
  7. Grab app: Southeast Asia's app for uber. food delivery, etc.
  8. Ferry/speedboat: $0.59-$4.44
  9. Plane (TL;DR - domestic flights can be cheap):
    1. Bangkok to Chiang Mai: nonstop 1 hour flight as low as ~$14.
    2. Bangkok to Chiang Rai: nonstop 1 hour flight as low as ~$14.
    3. Bangkok to Krabi: nonstop 1.5 hour flight as low as ~$14.
    4. Bangkok to Phuket: nonstop 1.5 hour flight as low as ~$14.
    5. Chiang Mai to Chiang Rai: no direct flights for some reason so a 4hr bus for ~$6-$12 or 3hr taxi for ~$60-$75.
    6. Chiang Mai to Phuket: nonstop 2hr flight for ~$90, or as low as $60.
    7. Chiang Rai to Phuket: not finding a nonstop flight at this time.
