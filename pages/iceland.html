Iceland
Activities
  1. Guided tour around the island with Troll Expeditions. With choices of a 6-day ($2180) or 8-day ($2549) ring road tour around Iceland, a guide can drive us around in a minibus to see all the main sights, and it includes a hotel + pick up/drop off.
  2. Then, pick one or two destinations where we relax and do our own thing for the remainder of our stay.

    3. Or, we can spend the 8-10 days doing everything self-guided.

  3. Golden Circle, Diamond Beach, lagoons, waterfalls, and glaciers. Northern Lights would be a bonus.
Food
  1. Similar to Norway, e.g. seafood and lamb are highlights.
  2. Troll Expeditions includes breakfast and we can be shuttled to dinner after each day's activities.
Lodging
  1. Included in the Troll Expeditions package.
  2. The Retreat at Blue Lagoon
  3. Hotel Orkin has 2 bedroom apartments for $140/night, twin bed rooms for $120/night, and triple bed rooms for $100/night.
  4. Nicer hotels in Reykjavik can run for $200/night.
  5. Nicer hotels in South Iceland (Hofn) run for about the same, $215/night.
  6. Arnanes Country Hotel - average nightly rate is ~$150. Double rooms that can sleep 4 for ~$170/night (~$40/person/night)
Transit
  1. Troll Expeditions includes transit in the price.
  2. Buses are available and affordable - ~$4 per single ride, and day passes are available.
  3. Rental cars
Cost
  1. Flight (roundtrip from SFO to Reykjavik KEF): $700-$1000 depending on layover time.
  2. Lodging: Let's assume an average of $100/night if we share rooms, and can go lower if more people share rooms.
  3. Transit: Maybe allocate $20/day for bus travel?
  4. Total (est. lower range) for a 10 day trip: $700 + $1000 + $200 = $1900, excluding food and other activities/entertainment.
  5. Total (est. upper range) for 6-day Troll Expeditions loop and 4 nights in 1-2 cities: $1000 + $2180 + $400 + $80 = $3660, excluding non-expedition food and other activities/entertainment.
