Norway
Activities
  1. Nature:
    1. Cruising, kayaking, and swimming in the fjords.
    2. Hiking - popular hikes include Trolltunga, Gaustatoppen, Besseggen Ridge, HM Queen Sonja's trail, and Aurlandsdalen. Trolltunga can be a backpacking destination.
    3. Northern Lights under the right fall/winter evening conditions.
    4. Glaciers
    5. More info on fjord cruise trips here.
  2. Other Sights:
    1. West Norwegian Fjords (also Nature) - World Heritage Cites (UNESCO)
    2. Oslo
    3. Bryggen, Bergen
    4. Tromso and Arctic Circle
    5. Viking Ship Museum
Food
  1. Seafood, deer, elk, and sheep/lamb. I hear Finnbiff and other stews are delicious.
  2. Maaemo, #47 on The World's 50 Best Restaurants (Michelen 3-star)
  3. Cheese

Cost
  1. Flight (from SFO to Oslo): $800-$1300 roundtrip, 1 layover ~2-3 hours. Alternatively, can fly into Tromso.
  2. Lodging:
    1. Cabin for 10 days: $2750
    2. Hotels ~$120-$180/night.
    3. AirBnB 10 people 10 days: $3000-$5000 total, or $300-$500/person
  3. Transit
    1. Public Transportation - daily pass ~$5/day, weekly pass ~$18/week
    2. Oslo's Ruter Transit Network: Oslo Pass = unlimited travel + museum access for 10 days for ~$58
    3. Long-distance Train: ~$30 for a 2-hour ride
  4. Total (est. range): $1300 - $2200. Depends on how many passes, lodging options selected, etc. Does not include food.
