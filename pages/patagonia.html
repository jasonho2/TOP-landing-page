Lodging
  1. Mid-range hotel option in Chile near Torres del Paine is ~$350/night if two people share a room.
  2. According to ChatGPT, staying in these EcoCamp Patagonia yurts would run $250-$450/night if two people share one, and includes breakfast and dinner. But to get true cost we need to inquire.
  3. Other options in Torres del Paine are Hosteria Pehoe, Hotel Lago Grey, hostels, refugios, or campgrounds. Alternatively we can stay in Puerto Natales and make our way up for the day(s).
  4. This AirBnB in Ushuaia is kind of insane... $2739 total for 3 nights. For 10 people would be $91/person...

    5. A lot of hospitality options also include food and tour/experience packages, which can be intriguiging if we're looking for a guide during part of the trip.
